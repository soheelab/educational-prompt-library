# 🏫 Integrated Unit Plan Generator

A comprehensive XML-style prompt for generating complete, 
standards-aligned integrated unit plans for primary classrooms.

---

## 📋 What This Prompt Does

Give it your grade level, standards, and schedule — it asks 
clarifying questions first, then generates a complete multi-week 
integrated unit plan including:

- Weekly lesson breakdowns (40-minute and 80-minute blocks)
- Spelling and vocabulary word lists
- Differentiation strategies for ELL students
- Assessment summaries
- Digital literacy progression
- Culturally responsive activities

---

## 🎯 Best For

- Grades 1-5 international or ESL classrooms
- Schools using Common Core, NGSS, and AERO standards
- Teachers who want AI to gather information before generating
- Anyone tired of generic "All About Me" units

---

## 🚀 How To Use It

1. Open `prompt.xml` and copy the entire contents
2. Paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini
3. The prompt will ask you questions before generating anything
   — just answer them
4. Receive your complete unit plan in markdown format
5. Paste into Google Docs for final formatting (you'll want to enable Markdown support under Tools>Preferences>Enable Markdown in Google Docs)

**Recommended model at Present:** Claude Sonnet (free tier works)
